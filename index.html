Our District

About Harlam Unified

Founded in 1953 at the corner of orange groves and opportunity, Harlam USD has grown into one of Orange County's most distinctive unified districts.

Mission

To prepare every student for college, career, and community life through rigorous academics and boldly specialized pathways you will not find anywhere else. Anywhere.

Vision

A district where a student can master oil painting before lunch and parallel drifting after — safely, legally, and with full academic credit.

Core Values

Excellence, curiosity, community, and the firm belief that "well-rounded" should be taken literally, like a skate bowl.

District Leadership

Office of the Superintendent

LAOfficial Portrait Pending

Lars Alexandersson, The Magician

Superintendent of Schools • Appointed 2023

Superintendent Alexandersson brings to Harlam USD an unconventional résumé of tactical leadership, international experience, and stage magic. Known throughout the district for pulling balanced budgets out of seemingly empty hats, he has made the impossible feel routine: three consecutive years of rising graduation rates, a fully funded arts expansion, and a dove appearing at every board meeting, origin unknown.

"There is no illusion in our results. The magic is the teachers. I just do the card tricks."

Under his leadership, Harlam USD has been recognized twice by the Orange County Department of Education for innovation in specialized pathways, and once by the International Brotherhood of Magicians for "Best Vanishing Achievement Gap (Regional)."
